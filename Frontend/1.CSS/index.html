Fixed Background Image with a Different Background Color

This is an example page demonstrating usage of a background image that stays fixed when you scroll the text content. The background color of the body is different, providing contrast behind the semi-transparent content box. The background image is sized specifically to 400px by 300px and positioned centrally.

Scroll down to see that the image remains stationary while the content moves.
